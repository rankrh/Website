AmeriCorps Ecological Crew Member

Northwest Service Corps

April 2018 - August 2018, Portland, OR

I volunteered with AmeriCorps as a crew member with the Northwest Service Corps for the summer of 2017. Working with a team of 8, we tackled ecological restoration initiatives in National Forests and Wildlife refuges throughout Oregon and Washington. A large part of what we did was finding, documenting, and removing invasive species, but we also planted a lot of trees as part of reforestation efforts. We worked with experts from the Department of the Interior and Department of Agriculture who taught us first-hand principles in modern forest ecology. A lot of the time my team and I worked in isolated, backcountry settings, which made us solve problems and interpret job guidelines without direct supervision.
